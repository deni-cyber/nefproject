Background

In 2020, in Nairobi, Kenya, a group of skilled individuals founded "North Edge Fabricators Ltd." With a passion for metalwork and a commitment to quality and innovation, they crafted iconic structures and artwork. Beyond their workshop, they uplifted the community by hiring and training from disadvantaged backgrounds. Their impact and artistry soon gained recognition, making North Edge a beacon of inspiration in the industry.
